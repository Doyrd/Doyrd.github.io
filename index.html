About me

My name is Dave Bakker, I have a passion for programming and you found the Junior game developer that you want on your team.

I've always loved working with code and I wanted to do more with programming, this is what led me to studying Media- and Gamedevelopment.
I'm currently a second year game developer student at Mediacollege in Amsterdam.

Three words to describe me are reliable, hard-working and creative.

My interests go towards C# and the Unity Engine, but I'm also trying to test with other engines, like the Unreal Engine and I would like to get more into using C++.

Other coding languages that I learned myself or got to learn by my study are HTML5, CSS, Java, JavaScript, GameMaker language, PHP and a little bit of mySQL.

Besides programming and learning myself new skills, I like to travel, watch Steven Spielberg movies and make creations or edits with Photoshop.

One of my current goals is to get an internship at a Dutch game company. I would also like to master the coding languages that I’m learning. I’m currently researching myself more into Technical artist, by creating my own low poly models and creating games related to that.

I recently quit my job working at local supermarket, so I'm open for any new opportunities.
